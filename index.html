About Me

Born in Tokyo, I grew up in Hamburg, Germany. I moved to the U.S. in the 1980s, and have lived in California, Oregon and Pennsylvania. Since leaving college, I've been a business consultant, program/project manager, veterinary technician, and many more. My current career goal is to complete the coding bootcamp and become a successful web developer.

I enjoy pet sitting on the side, and my favorite things are dogs. Some day, I'd like to help build technologies that will benefit our pets.
